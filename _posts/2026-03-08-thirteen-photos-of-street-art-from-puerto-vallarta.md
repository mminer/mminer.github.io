title: "Thirteen Photos of Street Art From Puerto Vallarta, Plus a Crocodile"

Puerto Vallarta, Mexico is a beautiful place. We got stranded for three days when cartel goons started [burning cars and the airport shut down](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/23/world/americas/peurto-vallarta-tourists-cartel.html), but otherwise it was a great trip. Lots to see and do — beaches and tacos and iguanas — and photo ops galore. Here are a few shots from Zona Romántica and Bucerías to the north.
